-> New updates

In Version 1.0.1, Rewrite has been integrated in Gmail. Now you can compose your emails quickly with a click of just one button. Enter your prompt as the subject and press the "Generate" button, your email body will be generate in seconds. Refine the email and you are done ! You can thus efficiently and quickly get your message without worrying about what to write or how to write !
